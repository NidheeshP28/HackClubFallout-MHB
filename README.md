I studied and taught myself on aerodynamics of a boomerang and tried to solve how I can make a Mario hat can act like a boomerang. I checked with Copilot to imagine seeing if it is possible to make Cappy (The name of the Mario Hat from Odyssey) act like a boomerang. To have aerodynamics to work for the cap and to meet all the aspects and concepts of a boomerang, the ideology was to merge the white ghost version of cappy and the version of when cappy became the Mario hat.

Mostly, just done brainstorming on the Cappy Boomerang.
